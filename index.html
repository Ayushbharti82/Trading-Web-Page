Bitcoin: Digital Gold

Bitcoin (BTC) revolutionized finance as the first decentralized cryptocurrency. With a fixed supply of 21 million coins, it's often called "digital gold." Traders analyze BTC's price action for market trends, as it often leads altcoin movements. Key strategies include swing trading during volatility or long-term holding (HODLing). The 2024 halving reduced miner rewards, historically triggering bull runs due to supply shocks.

Risk Management

Trading Psychology

Emotions destroy traders. Greed leads to overtrading; fear causes missed opportunities. Stick to your plan: set stop-losses and take-profits before entering a trade. Diversify across crypto (BTC, ETH), stocks, and commodities. Use dollar-cost averaging (DCA) to reduce volatility impact. As Warren Buffett says, "Be fearful when others are greedy, and greedy when others are fearful."
